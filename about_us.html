Wondering who we are?

CossiePlay is a family-run business, operating in Hulkstown since 2012. Our passion for cosplay has led us to create outstanding custom-made costumes. Cosplayers and customers of fancy dress can order any kind of costume - from a simple Spiderman outfit, a wild Elf or even costumes featuring some more advanced and exciting features such as; armour, animatronics, realistic weaponry and work force-fields(!). Of course we also do cosplay based on game characters, we can almost anything!

We are also proud to host the monthly "CossiePlay Party" at the town hall, so make sure you check it out!

But who is who at CossiePlay? Well our family includes:

Steven Rogers

– Steve was a Navy Seal for 37 years before getting interested in hosting parties and helping people enjoy their fantasies together in a family-friendly fun environment.

Sally Rogers

– After only 6 weeks of her first dress-making course, Sally was already designing and sewing elaborate ball gowns and costumes that amazed her instructors. 10 years later and she's making even more amazing creations for happy customers.
